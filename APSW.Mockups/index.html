Overview

What is the Structural Warranty or Builders Warranty

A structural warranty, also known as a builders warranty, is an agreement between a builder and homebuyer that helps protect both the builder and homebuyer against possible construction defects. It sets clear expectations on both parties about what is covered what isn't covered in the builders warranty. Having a structural warranty can significantly reduce financial risks for both parties.
